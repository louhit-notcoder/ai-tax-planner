# Phase completion matrix

“Complete” is separated into **code complete** and **release evidence complete**.
Code cannot substitute for a CA opinion, legal opinion, penetration test, cloud account
or controlled pilot.

## Complex tax coverage

NRI/RNOR, foreign income/assets, Schedule FA/FSI/TR, Form 67/FTC, business,
presumptive income, F&O, VDA, unlisted assets and complex losses have typed inputs,
schedule data and deterministic/review pathways. They remain `PROVISIONAL`, `BLOCKED`
or expert-review-only when the evidence or CA-certified rule coverage is insufficient.
The system never converts “not certified” into zero income or a final export.

# Tax scope and release policy

## Automated V1 target

Resident individual, ITR-1 and selected ITR-2: salary/pension, up to two house
properties, common other sources/deductions, listed Indian equity and selected
mutual funds, tax payments, AIS/TIS/26AS reconciliation and old/new regime.

## Modelled but expert-review/fail-closed

NRI/RNOR, foreign assets/income, FA/FSI/TR, Form 67/FTC, foreign ESOP/RSU,
property gains, unlisted shares, VDA, F&O, business/professional income,
presumptive tax, complex losses/agricultural income/clubbing and non-individuals.

The engine may calculate review drafts for some of these records but cannot mark
an unsupported or incompletely evidenced scenario final. CA-approved golden-case
certification controls feature-flag activation.
